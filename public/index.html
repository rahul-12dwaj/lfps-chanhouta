      Little Flower Public School Chanhouta

      Online Admission Session (2024-2025)

      Dr. Suresh Shah

      Principal, LFPS Chanhouta

      Dear Students, Welcome to Little Flower Public School, Chanhouta! It is with immense pleasure and excitement that I extend a warm welcome to each and every one of you. As you step into our school, you are embarking on a journey filled with boundless opportunities for growth, learning, and discovery. Here at Little Flower, we believe in nurturing not just academic excellence, but also character, creativity, and compassion. Our dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and vibrant community are here to support you every step of the way. As you settle into our school, remember that you are not just students; you are valued members of our Little Flower family. Embrace every challenge, cherish every moment, and strive to be the best version of yourself. Together, let us embark on this incredible journey of learning, friendship, and personal growth. I look forward to getting to know each and every one of you and witnessing the remarkable achievements that lie ahead. Once again, welcome to Little Flower Public School, where every dream finds wings to soar! With warm regards, Dr. Suresh Shah Principal, Little Flower Public School, Chanhouta
